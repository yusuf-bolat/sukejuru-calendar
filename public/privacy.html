Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 18, 2025

Student Calendar (the “App”) helps users manage schedules and optionally export events to Google Calendar. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your choices.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

Who We Are

Controller/Operator: Student Calendar

Contact: yusufbolat004@gmail.com

Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address and profile name via Supabase Authentication.
  • Calendar data you create: event title, description, start/end date-time, all‑day flag, and color.
  • AI assistant inputs: messages you type to generate or edit events.
  • AI memory summary: a minimal, per‑user summary of recurring activities stored as structured data (e.g., activity name, typical weekday/time) to assist with follow‑ups and pronoun resolution.
  • Technical/diagnostic data: logs and error information to maintain reliability.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate you and provide your personal calendar.
  • Create, update, and delete your events in our database (Supabase).
  • Assist with scheduling via the AI chatbot and memory summary feature.
  • Export events to your Google Calendar when you explicitly request it.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the App (including troubleshooting).

AI and OpenAI Usage

The App uses an API call from our server to the OpenAI service to interpret your chat requests and return structured scheduling data. We do not expose your API key in the browser. As of OpenAI’s current policy, content submitted via the API is not used to train or improve OpenAI models by default. For details, see OpenAI API Data Usage.

  • Your chat prompts are transmitted to OpenAI solely to provide the requested scheduling assistance.
  • We do not use chat content for advertising or marketing.
  • The per‑user memory summary we store is a compact representation of recurring activities to improve your experience.

Google Calendar API Limited Use

When you connect Google Calendar, we use the scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events only to insert events you ask us to create. In line with the Google API Services User Data Policy (including Limited Use):

  • We access Google user data only to provide the requested feature (creating events).
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to provide or improve the feature, comply with law, or with your consent.
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising or marketing.
  • You can revoke access any time at Google Account Permissions.

Legal Bases (where applicable)

  • Consent (e.g., connecting Google Calendar).
  • Contract/Service delivery (providing your calendar and related features).
  • Legitimate interests (security, reliability, improving the App).

Sharing and Service Providers

We do not sell your personal data. We use service providers to operate the App:

  • Supabase (database, authentication)
  • Vercel (hosting)
  • OpenAI (AI processing)
  • Google (Calendar API when you connect)

Data Location and Transfers

Data may be stored or processed in the regions where our providers operate. Where required, appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) are used.

Security

We use HTTPS, Supabase Row Level Security, and server‑side storage of API keys. No method is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect information.

Retention and Deletion

  • You can delete events in the App at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account or the memory summary via the contact email.
  • Backups and logs may persist for a limited period for reliability and security.

Your Rights

Subject to your location and applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email above.

Children

Changes to This Policy
