Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

Student Calendar ("the App") helps users manage schedules and optionally export events to Google Calendar. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: your email and profile name via Supabase Authentication.
  • Calendar data you create in the App: event title, description, start/end date-time, all-day flag, and color.
  • Chat inputs you type for the scheduling assistant (processed to help create events).
  • Technical data: basic logs (e.g., errors) to maintain the service.

How We Use Data

  • To authenticate you and provide your personal calendar.
  • To create, update, and delete your events stored in our database (Supabase).
  • To export events to your Google Calendar when you explicitly request it.
  • To improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

Google Calendar API Limited Use

When you connect Google Calendar, we use the scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events only to insert events you ask us to create. In line with the Google API Services User Data Policy (including the Limited Use requirements):

  • We only access and use Google user data to provide the requested features (creating events).
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to provide or improve the feature, comply with law, or with your consent.
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising or marketing.
  • You can revoke access at any time at Google Account Permissions.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data may be processed by our service providers (e.g., Supabase, Vercel) to operate the App.

Security

We use Supabase Row Level Security and HTTPS to protect data. No method is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard information.

Retention and Deletion

You can delete events from within the App. You can also contact us to request account or data deletion.

Your Choices

  • Sign out at any time from the App.
  • Revoke Google permissions at Google Account Permissions.
  • Contact us for data questions or removal requests.
